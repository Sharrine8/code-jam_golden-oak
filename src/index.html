Autumn-Themed Breakfast Recipes

Delicious morning choices for the whole family!

  • Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal

    These warm oats sweetened with brown sugar and seasonal apple are just the thing to get your family out of bed. Best of all, they only take 15 minutes to make!

  • Pumpkin Pancakes

    A stack of these seasonal pancakes is sure to get you out of bed in the morning! Not only are they made with real pumpkin puree, but they also have plenty of pumpkin pie spice!

  • Pumpkin Spice Overnight Oats

    Overnight oats are a lifesave on busy mornings--and they're even better when they're loaded with pumpkin spice flavor! You can prep them the night before and stash them in jars in your fridge.
